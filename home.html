About Us

Explore Baguio is your go to guide for experiencing the charm and beauty of the City of Pines. With its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and rich heritage, Baguio offers a refreshing escape for all kinds of travelers.

Our website highlights must see destinations like Burnham Park, Mines View, Wright Park, and many more. Whether you're a nature lover, an art enthusiast, or simply looking for peaceful views, there’s something here for you.

We also feature some of Baguio’s most beloved local delicacies from strawberry taho to strawberry jam and other homegrown and unique food of baguio. These flavors capture the heart of the city and offer a true taste of Baguio culture.

From famous spots to hidden treasures, Explore Baguio is your companion in discovering what makes this city truly unforgettable.
